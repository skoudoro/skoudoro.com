A personal diary documenting my journey learning 10m air pistol shooting. I started from zero with no prior experience and decided to log everything — training sessions, competition results, gear notes, and the mental side of precision shooting.

The site is a simple static blog where I track progress over time, reflect on what works and what doesn't, and occasionally share resources for other beginners getting into the sport.
