A Google Workspace add-on that eliminates double bookings by consolidating multiple external calendar sources — Outlook, Airbnb, Booking.com, Yahoo Calendar — into Google Calendar.

Updates range from every 3 hours on the free plan to real-time on paid tiers. Available on the Google Workspace Marketplace.
